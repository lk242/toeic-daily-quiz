## Part 2 模擬 - 應答問題

**答題技巧：注意問句開頭的疑問詞（Who/What/When/Where/Why/How），答案要對應問題類型。**

### Q1: When is the meeting scheduled?
- (A) In the conference room.
- (B) At 2 o'clock this afternoon.
- (C) Yes, I attended the meeting.

### Q2: Who is responsible for this project?
- (A) It's due next Friday.
- (B) Ms. Park is in charge.
- (C) The project is going well.

### Q3: Where should I submit this document?
- (A) By the end of the day.
- (B) It's a financial report.
- (C) To the front desk on the second floor.

### Q4: Would you like to attend the conference?
- (A) It was held last week.
- (B) Yes, I'd be happy to.
- (C) The conference room is upstairs.

### Q5: How many employees are in the department?
- (A) The department is on the third floor.
- (B) About twenty-five.
- (C) Yes, they are all employees.

## 答案與解析

### Part 5 答案
1. **(B) department** — 空格需要名詞當主詞。department（部門）
2. **(A) submit** — must 後面接原形動詞
3. **(B) colleagues** — his 後面接名詞，asked 的對象是複數同事
4. **(B) available** — is not 後面接形容詞
5. **(C) postponed** — has been + 過去分詞（被動語態）
6. **(A) confirm** — Please 後面接原形動詞（祈使句）
7. **(C) attend** — required to 後面接原形動詞
8. **(D) supervisor** — The 後面需要名詞當主詞
9. **(C) experience** — years of 後面接不可數名詞
10. **(D) by** — by Friday 表示「在星期五之前」
11. **(A) organize** — need to 後面接原形動詞
12. **(B) appointed** — was + 過去分詞（被動語態）
13. **(B) employs** — 主詞 The company 是第三人稱單數，現在式加 s
14. **(C) responsibilities** — specific 後面接名詞
15. **(D) meeting** — The 後面需要名詞

### Part 1 答案
1. **(B)** — 女性坐在辦公桌前，sitting at a desk 正確
2. **(B)** — 兩人握手，shaking hands 正確
3. **(A)** — 男人在操作影印機，using the copy machine 正確

### Part 2 答案
1. **(B)** — When 問時間，回答具體時間
2. **(B)** — Who 問人，回答人名
3. **(C)** — Where 問地點，回答具體地點
4. **(B)** — Would you like 是邀請，Yes 回答合理
5. **(B)** — How many 問數量，回答數字
